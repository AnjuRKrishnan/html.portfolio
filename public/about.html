About Me

At Infosys, developed and delivered multiple web applications for various domains, using Java and Angular. I also have a strong background in back end development, with skills in relational databases and web technologies. I graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering from Adhiyamaan College in 2020.

I am passionate about learning new technologies and enhancing my skills to deliver high-quality solutions that meet the needs and goals of the customers and the business. I value collaboration, innovation, and feedback, and I strive to contribute to the growth and success of the organizations I work with.
